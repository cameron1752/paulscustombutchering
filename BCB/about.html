About Us

Get to know us

We pride ourselves on reliable delivery, quality products, and accommodating the special needs of our customers. Our current customer base consists of delivery to campgrounds, convenient stores, delis, farm and garden stores, and traditional bait and tackle shops. We deliver to our locally based PA stops on Wednesday, NY Area on Thursdays, and further reaching areas of PA on Fridays. Weekend deliveries are not normally available, however, there are certain occasions in which we make exceptions in order to meet special needs of our clients.

Where we come from

Our family has been in the live bait industry for over 70 years, passed down among three generations. Theodore Knickerbocker started out in a little shop in Mt. Cobb selling hand-picked worms to the public. His son Ted Knickerbocker continued this for many years, eventually growing to serve only as a wholesale distributor, well known and loved in the local industry. Before Ted’s passing in 2008, he passed down his knowledge and some of his customer base to James Webster who is the face of Butternut Creek Bait, with Ted’s daughter, Denise Knickerbocker, hiding in the wings administratively. James has since taken on many more new clients, serving a total of 72 clients in the region.
